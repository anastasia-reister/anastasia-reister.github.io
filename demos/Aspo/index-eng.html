ASPO is a metal manufacturing company fabricating metal components and ready to use products made to order for virtually any range of customers. With over 25 years in business, we have the experience and expertise to take on jobs of all sizes, it's no wonder we've become the leading custom metal fabricator in the Republic of Moldova. The size of production and warehouse facilities grants our company the ability to satisfy equally well the needs of a private project as well as of an industrial-scale enterprise. We've earned trust of our customers and built a client base of over a 1000 enterprises in Moldova and beyond. At the moment from the company warehouse, ASPO is ready to offer more than 300 types of metal-roll products. We continue to maintain our position as a leader in the specialty fabricating industry by constantly improving with continuous education and equipment update. We welcome any kind of cooperation and ready to consider your offers.
